Our story

We built a vault for the moments that matter.

Aeterna was born from a simple question: what if you could send a message to the future? Not a tweet — a sealed, encrypted vault of everything you feel right now.

Why we exist

Every generation loses its memories. Hard drives fail. Platforms shut down. Photos fade. The things that matter most are the most fragile.

We built Aeterna to fix that. A place where memories don't just get stored — they get preserved, encrypted, and delivered exactly when you need them most.

Our mission is simple: make the present moment permanent.

"The present moment always will have been. We just make sure you can find it again."
— Aeterna founding philosophy

Timeline

How we got here

2024

The idea

Founded after a hard drive failure erased five years of family photos. We asked: why doesn't something better exist?

2025

First capsule sealed

Beta launched with 50 users. First capsule sealed: a letter from a mother to her newborn daughter, to open in 18 years.

2026

Public launch

Aeterna opens to everyone. Pay-as-you-go pricing introduced. AES-256 encryption on every capsule by default.
