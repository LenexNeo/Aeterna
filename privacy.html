Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026  ·  Effective: April 5, 2026  ·  Aeterna Digital Ltd, Colombo, Sri Lanka
Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. We are committed to full transparency.

Table of contents

1. Information we collect 2. How we use your information 3. Data storage & security 4. Encryption & technical security 5. Data sharing 6. Your rights 7. Cookies 8. Data retention 9. Contact us

1. Information we collect

When you use Aeterna, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash — we never store plain-text passwords).
  • Payment information: Processed securely via PayPal or Stripe. We do not store your card details on our servers.
  • Capsule content: Files, photos, videos, and text you upload are stored encrypted. Only you can access your capsule content.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, browser type, and device information for improving the service.
  • Communications: Messages you send us via the contact form.

2. How we use your information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the Aeterna service:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process payments for capsule purchases
  • To store and retrieve your capsule content securely
  • To send unlock notifications when your capsule date arrives
  • To respond to your support enquiries
  • To improve the platform based on usage patterns

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Data storage & security

All data is stored on secure servers. Your capsule content is physically stored in encrypted format — meaning even our team cannot read your personal files or messages.

We maintain regular backups to ensure your memories are never lost. Backup copies are also encrypted using the same AES-256 standard.

4. Encryption & technical security

Aeterna uses industry-standard security measures:

  • AES-256 encryption: All capsule content is encrypted using Advanced Encryption Standard with 256-bit keys — the same standard used by banks and governments.
  • TLS/SSL: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3.
  • Password hashing: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt — we cannot recover your password if lost.
  • Secure payments: Card payments are processed by PCI-DSS compliant payment providers (PayPal, Stripe). We never see or store your card number.
  • Access controls: Only the capsule owner can access their capsule content, enforced at both application and database levels.

5. Data sharing

We share data with the following third parties only as necessary to operate the service:

  • Payment processors: PayPal and Stripe process payment data under their own privacy policies.
  • Email service: Used solely to send you transactional emails (order confirmations, unlock notifications).
  • Legal obligations: We may disclose data if required by law or court order.

We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties.

6. Your rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability: Download all your capsule content at any time.
  • Objection: Opt out of any non-essential data processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@aeterna.io

7. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the service to function:

  • Session cookies: Keep you logged in during your visit.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings.

We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

8. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Capsule content is stored until the unlock date has passed and you have downloaded your content, or until you request deletion.

If you delete your account, all associated data including capsule content will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

9. Contact us

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact our Data Protection Officer:

  • Email: privacy@aeterna.io
  • Address: Aeterna Digital Ltd, Colombo, Western Province, Sri Lanka
  • Response time: Within 5 business days
