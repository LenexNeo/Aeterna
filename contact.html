Quick answers

Common questions

How do I access my capsule?

Log in and visit "My Capsules". Sealed capsules show a countdown timer. Unlocked ones have an Open button.

Can I get a refund?

Yes — full refund within 30 days of purchase if the capsule has not been sealed with content yet.

What if I forget my password?

Use the "Forgot password" link on the login page. A reset link will be sent to your registered email address.

What file types can I upload?

Photos (JPG, PNG, WEBP), videos (MP4, MOV), audio (MP3, WAV), and documents (PDF, TXT, DOCX).

Can I change the unlock date?

Yes — you can extend the unlock date any time before it arrives. You cannot move it to an earlier date once sealed.

Is my data secure?

All capsules are encrypted with AES-256. Only you can access your capsule — not even our team can read your data.
