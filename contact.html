Quick answers

Common questions

How do I access my capsule?

Log in and visit "My Capsules". Sealed capsules show a countdown. Unlocked ones have an Open button.

Can I get a refund?

Yes — full refund within 30 days of purchase if the capsule has not been sealed with content yet.

What if I forget my password?

Use the "Forgot password" link on the login page. A reset link will be sent to your registered email.
