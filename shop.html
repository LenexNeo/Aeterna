FAQ

Common questions

What happens if I need more storage later?

You can purchase an additional capsule with more storage. Each capsule is independent — buy as many as you need.

Can I change the unlock date after purchasing?

Yes — you can extend the unlock date any time before it arrives. You cannot move it to an earlier date once sealed.

What file types can I upload?

Photos (JPG, PNG, WEBP), videos (MP4, MOV), audio (MP3, WAV), and text documents (PDF, TXT, DOCX).

Is my data safe and private?

Yes. All capsules are encrypted with AES-256 before storage. Only you can access your capsule — not even our team.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase, provided the capsule has not yet been sealed with content.

Do I need to create an account?

Yes — an account is required to store, manage, and access your capsules. Registration is free and takes under a minute.
